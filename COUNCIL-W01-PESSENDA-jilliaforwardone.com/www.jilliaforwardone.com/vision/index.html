Jillia’s Vision For Ward One

Now more than ever, we need a proactive leader at City Hall who will take action and drive progressive issues forward. Jillia is that person. She will be a champion for the residents of Ward One. She will amplify our voices and be the advocate we need on the City Council.

Jillia will:

Fight for working families

 

Jillia will be a progressive champion for working families. She’ll fight:

  • For a minimum wage of $15/hour
  • For stable workplace protections, like secure schedules
  • To prosecute perpetrators of wage theft

 

Keep Northeast an affordable place to live

Ward 1 is growing and we need to ensure that rising housing costs don’t push people out. Jillia will be a strong advocate for:

  • More mixed income housing. She will support strategies to ensure mixed income neighborhoods and require affordable units in market-rate developments.
  • Removing obstacles to building affordable housing and innovative housing types like tiny homes, co-housing, and intentional communities
  • Protecting housing that is currently affordable and strengthen tenant rights

 

Invest in our neighborhoods

Jillia will be a champion at City Hall for:

  • Tackling climate change and investing in a local food economy
  • Finding proactive improvements for dangerous intersections in Northeast and the risk posed by oil trains
  • Investing in the arts and advancing opportunities for women, artists of color and LGBTQ artists
  • Fight for healthy neighborhoods over big polluters
  • Revitalizing commercial corridors with high-quality streetscapes and public spaces
  • Prioritizing people on foot, on bikes, and public transportation

 

Help small businesses thrive

Small businesses are the backbone of a thriving community. Jillia knows this and will put small business owners ahead of large corporations. This includes creating a permanent Small Business Office at city hall. Jillia will:

  • Support co-ops, small businesses and businesses owned by people of color and women
  • Help small businesses navigate the City’s processes
  • Vote against handouts for big business,including, the tax-payer funded U.S. Bank Stadium

 

Jillia knows that trust in local government and elected leaders is critical for building a strong democracy. Jillia will:

  • Support publicly funded elections
  • Monitor police use of force and hold officers accountable for excessive use of force; prioritize de-escalation training
  • Decriminalize addiction and mental health issues and invest in support services

 

Build trust and accountability at City Hall

Jillia is dedicated to eliminating racial disparities in education, employment and housing. Effective government requires justice and equity at the core of everything that we do. Jillia is committed to an integrated policy platform that is centered around racial justice. It is not just a buzzword for her.  

 

“I am ready to get to work as a progressive champion for Ward 1.”
