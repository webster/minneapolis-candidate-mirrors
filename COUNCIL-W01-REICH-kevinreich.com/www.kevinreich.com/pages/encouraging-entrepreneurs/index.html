Encouraging entrepreneurs

Outside of the downtown neighborhoods, Ward One has been a leader in significant economic investment in Minneapolis. Over $90 million in revenue bonds helped two major businesses relocate their operations in NE, bringing 1,000 jobs to the area and creating 800 more new jobs in the years ahead. In addition, nearly $500K in small business loans and financing has been dispersed to support local companies.
