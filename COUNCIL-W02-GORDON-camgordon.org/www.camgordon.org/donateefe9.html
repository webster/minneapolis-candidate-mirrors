Contribute

In accordance with campaign contribution limits set by the State of Minnesota for 2017, Cam Gordon does not accept more than $600 from individuals.

Cam holds himself to the high ethical standards of the Green Party, and will not accept donations from Political Action Committees.  In addition, the campaign will not accept donations from paid lobbyists who lobby at City Hall or from anyone with a pending matter that will be acted on by the City Council, or that has been acted on by the Council within six months.

Contributions are not tax deductible and, because this is a municipal election, do not qualify for the Minnesota Political Contribution Refund Program.
