Cam Votes for Progressive City Budget

12/07/2016

The Council has adopted a responsible, responsive, future focused 2017 budget. Cam fought to include significant funding for community and health-based initiatives to prevent violence, better and more targeted support through a new small business office, funding for affordable housing, growing the green business matching grant program, funding for the Clean Energy Partnership and other clean energy programs, capital funding for new bikeways and sidewalks, and much more.

Roundtable Discussion on Trees

09/25/2013

Cam is hosting a roundtable discussion about the threats and opportunities facing our urban tree canopy on Monday, October 7, from 7-8:30pm at Longfellow Park, 3435 36th Ave S.

We will discuss the tree loss from this summer’s storm, Emerald Ash Borer, the importance of watering trees, and the Longfellow Trees program. For more about Longfellow Trees, go to www.longfellow.org/trees.
