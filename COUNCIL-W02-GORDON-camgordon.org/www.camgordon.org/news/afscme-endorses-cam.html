AFSCME Endorses Cam!

02/16/2013

The American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 5, has endorsed Cam Gordon for reelection in Ward 2. Cam is a strong believer in the power of organized labor to make our city, country, and world a better place for all workers.

Endorsements

Cam Gordon is endorsed by the following organizations:

  • 5th District Green Party
  • AFSCME Council 5
  • Minneapolis Regional Labor Federation
  • Sierra Club North Star Chapter
  • SEIU Minnesota State Council
  • TakeAction Minnesota
